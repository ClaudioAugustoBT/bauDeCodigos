Monstros de Bolso
==================

Criar dispositivo (monstrodex) para cadastrar monstros de bolso, contendo os seguintes campos:

numero, tipo1, tipo2, hp, atk, def, spAtck. spDef, Speed

O Monstrodex deverá contemplar o CRUD completo para os mosntros de bolso, splash screen e DataGrid com os monstros cadastrados.


Site:
1) bulbapedia
2)https://pokemondb.net/pokedex/shiny

NORMAL, FIRE, WATER, ELECTRIC, GRASS, ICE, FIGHTING, POISON, GROUND, FLYING, PSYCHIC, BUG, ROCK, GHOST, DRAGON, DARK, STEEL, FAIRY

select cd_poke as NUMERO, nm_poke as NOME, t.nm_tipo as tipo1, t2.nm_tipo as tipo2
	from tb_poke as p inner join tb_tipo as t
		on t.cd_tipo = p.cd_tipo1
			inner join tb_tipo as t2
				on t2.cd_tipo = p.cd_tipo2;
